LIFE
570 CE

Birth

Born in Mecca. Orphaned at age six, raised by his grandfather and uncle in the desert, learning resilience.

595 CE

Marriage

Married Khadija, a noble merchant. A union of love and respect. He was known as Al-Amin for his honesty.

610 CE

Revelation

In the Cave of Hira, Gabriel commanded "Read!". The divine silence broke, marking the start of Prophethood.

613 CE

The Call

Began preaching publicly, calling for social justice and the worship of One God, challenging the elite.

619 CE

Sorrow

The 'Year of Sorrow'. Lost his beloved wife Khadija and uncle Abu Talib. Endured with immense patience.

622 CE

Hijrah

Migration to Medina. A journey of faith that established the first community and the Islamic Calendar.

624-627 CE

Struggle

Defended the community at Badr, Uhud, and the Trench against overwhelming odds, seeking peace where possible.

628 CE

Peace

Treaty of Hudaybiyyah. A strategic truce prioritizing peace over conflict, allowing the message to spread.

630 CE

Mercy

Conquest of Mecca. Entered peacefully. Granted general amnesty to former enemies: "Go, for you are free."

632 CE

Farewell

Delivered the final sermon: "An Arab has no superiority over a non-Arab... except by piety."
